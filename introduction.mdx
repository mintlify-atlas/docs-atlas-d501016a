## Your journey into paranormal investigation starts here

Ghost Planet is a free, community-driven platform that empowers anyone to conduct, document, and share paranormal investigations. Whether you're using a mobile device in the field or organizing evidence on desktop, Ghost Planet provides the tools you need to explore the unexplained.

<Note>
Ghost Planet is currently in active development. Features and functionality are being continuously improved based on community feedback.
</Note>

## Investigations

Investigations are the heart of Ghost Planet. Each investigation includes:

- **Location tracking**: Link to existing community locations or create private ones
- **Phenomena documentation**: Record what you're investigating
- **Evidence collection**: Attach multiple types of evidence
- **Safety features**: Add emergency contact information
- **Privacy controls**: Choose between public and private visibility

<Info>
Investigations connect locations, evidence, and users into a complete record of your paranormal research.
</Info>

## Evidence

Ghost Planet supports multiple evidence types to capture your findings:

### Text notes
Document observations, impressions, and context during investigations.

```json
{
  "subject": "Cold spot in master bedroom",
  "body": "Temperature dropped approximately 15 degrees near the northeast corner. Lasted approximately 2 minutes before dissipating.",
  "location_id": 123,
  "created_at": "2026-03-01T20:15:00Z"
}
```

### Photos
Capture visual evidence with captions and metadata.

- Supported formats: JPEG, JPG, PNG, WebP
- Maximum file size: 5 MB
- Automatic thumbnail generation

### Audio recordings
Record ambient sounds, investigator notes, and potential EVPs (Electronic Voice Phenomena).

- Maximum file size: 5 MB
- Duration tracking
- EVP classification flag

<Warning>
All evidence requires a valid location association and respects the investigation's visibility settings.
</Warning>

## Locations

Locations form a shared knowledge base of investigated sites:

**Location information includes:**
- Name and full address
- GPS coordinates (latitude/longitude)
- Historical lore and background stories
- Past investigation records
- Popularity metrics
- Public or private visibility

**Coordinate validation:**
- Latitude must be between -90 and 90
- Longitude must be between -180 and 180

<Tip>
Contribute verified locations to the community database to help other investigators discover new sites.
</Tip>

## Modes

### Mobile mode
Optimized for field work:
- Camera access for photos and video
- Microphone for audio notes and EVP capture
- Quick text note entry
- Real-time evidence upload

### Desktop mode
Comprehensive evidence management:
- Bulk file uploads (photos, audio, video)
- Webcam for investigator documentation
- Interactive map interface (Mappy)
- Location database browser with detailed cards
- Investigation organization and review

## Community values

Ghost Planet is built on principles of accessibility, respect, and scientific curiosity:

- **Free and accessible**: Available in standard and low-bandwidth formats
- **Welcoming community**: No tolerance for hateful or condescending language
- **Evidence-based**: Focus on replicable methods and productive knowledge sharing
- **Privacy-respecting**: Control your data with public/private visibility options
- **Safety-first**: Emergency contact features and responsible investigation practices

<Info>
Verified community members can contribute to public locations after demonstrating positive engagement through their investigations and community activity.
</Info>
